How it works

  1. The host splits everyone into two random teams.
  2. Each round shows one question with four possible answers.
  3. Talk it over as a team, then one of you taps the answer. There's one vote per team, so agree first.
  4. Once both teams have voted, the correct answer and a short explanation are shown.
  5. A correct answer scores one point. The first team to reach 3 points (and ahead) wins.

It's about discussing and reasoning together — not about being the fastest.
